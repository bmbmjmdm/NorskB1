PRIVACY POLICY — Norsk B1

Last updated: 2026-06-28

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Norsk B1" mobile application
(the "App", bundle identifier com.norskb1) handles information. Please
replace the contact details marked [ ... ] below before publishing.


SUMMARY

Norsk B1 collects no personal data. The App works entirely offline. Everything
it stores — your study progress, your settings, and any flashcards you add —
stays on your device. None of it is transmitted to the developer or to any
third party, and the developer has no ability to access it.


1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

The App does not:
  - require an account, sign-in, name, email address, or phone number;
  - use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs;
  - contain third-party SDKs that collect data;
  - access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera;
  - request any runtime permissions in the released app.


2. INFORMATION STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

To make the App work, the following is saved locally on your device only,
using the operating system's standard on-device storage:
  - study progress (which cards you have seen and their review schedule);
  - your settings (e.g. grading intervals, English-front percentage);
  - any flashcards you create within the App.

This information never leaves your device. We cannot see it. It is not backed
up to any server we control. (Your device's own OS backup, such as iCloud or
Google backup, may include it according to your device settings; that is under
your control, not ours.)


3. HOW THE INFORMATION IS USED

On-device data is used solely to provide the App's functionality — scheduling
reviews and remembering your preferences. It is not used for any other purpose.


4. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

Your on-device data remains until you delete it. You can remove it at any time
by using "Reset all progress" inside the App, or by uninstalling the App, which
deletes all of its locally stored data.


5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is a language-learning tool suitable for general audiences. Because the
App collects no personal information from anyone, it collects no personal
information from children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.


6. NETWORK USE

The released App does not connect to the internet and requests no network
permission on Android. It contains no servers, no remote APIs, and no remote
content.


7. THIRD PARTIES

The App does not share data with third parties because it does not collect or
transmit any data.


8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same location
where you found this document, with a new "Last updated" date.
